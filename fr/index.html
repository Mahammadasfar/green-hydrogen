WHO

ARE WE?

Founded in 2004, ACWA Power is a Saudi national champion and global leader in energy transition. We are the world’s largest private water desalination company, leader in energy transition and first mover into green hydrogen. With 9.5 million m³/day of desalinated water and 78.8 GW of power capacity under our portfolio, we provide power and clean water, reliably and responsibly to millions of people around the world.

Investemnt

ACWA Power launched its USD 1.2 billion IPO in 2021 and was listed on the Saudi Stock Exchange (Tadawul). Backed by the Public Investment Fund (PIF), we are investing in green hydrogen production, with landmark projects and global export plans already underway.

WHY GREEN

HYDROGEN

Aligned with Saudi Arabia’s Vision 2030 targets, ACWA Power is leveraging its renewable energy expertise to build a cleaner, more sustainable world. As the Kingdom moves towards becoming a hub for sustainability and innovation, ACWA Power is committed to diversifying the economy, and helping the countries in which we operate. Through investing in green hydrogen, we are meeting global demands of international markets, thus reducing the dependence on fossil fuels.

GREEN

HYDROGEN AT SCALE

ACWA Power is co-developing the USD 8.4 billion NEOM Green Hydrogen Company (NGHC), a joint venture between ACWA Power, Air Products and NEOM. Located in northwest Saudi Arabia, the plant will produce 600 tonnes/day of carbon-free hydrogen, powered entirely by renewable energy, which will be converted into 1.2 million tonnes of green ammonia annually.

Timeline

Commercial operations begin in 2026

Export model

Supplying green ammonia to global markets, especially Europe and Asia

Aim

Supports the decarbonisation efforts in transport and heavy industries, removing the dependence on fossil fuels

Global Footprint

In addition to our green hydrogen efforts in Saudi Arabia, ACWA Power has recently developed green hydrogen partnerships and agreements with Egypt, Jordan, Tunisia, Uzbekistan, Indonesia and the Netherlands.
